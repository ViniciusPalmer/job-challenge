# Product

## Register

product

## Users

High school students using a lightweight animal lookup tool during homework, classroom research, or personal curiosity sessions. They need quick answers without feeling like they are inside a dry institutional portal or a dense reference database.

## Product Purpose

This product helps students search for animals in English and discover concise, engaging information quickly. It should make exploration feel approachable and rewarding, with enough clarity to support school use while still encouraging curiosity-driven browsing.

## Brand Personality

Calm, academic, approachable. The interface should feel trustworthy and study-friendly without becoming stiff, clinical, or overly formal.

## Anti-references

- Generic school portal dashboards, especially flat administrative layouts that feel bureaucratic or dated.
- Dense encyclopedia-style experiences that overwhelm students with long text walls before they can orient themselves.

## Design Principles

- Make discovery feel easy: students should feel invited to explore, not forced through a heavy research workflow.
- Lead with clarity: the primary search and result signals should be obvious at a glance.
- Support learning without stiffness: the product can feel academic and credible while still remaining visually welcoming.
- Reward curiosity: browsing, scanning, and opening details should feel lightweight enough to encourage a second search.
- Keep information digestible: present knowledge in clear chunks that reduce intimidation for younger learners.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Prioritize color contrast so content remains readable and scannable across the core search and results flows.
